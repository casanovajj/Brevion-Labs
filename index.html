How It Works

A clear, guided path from choosing a project track to publishing a portfolio that admissions officers can instantly understand.

1

Choose Your Track

Tell us your track or Idea that matches your interests, current skills, and target universities/majors.

2

Get Curated Projects

You receive a curated list of project/Personalised project options with timelines, milestones, and expected outcomes.

3

Build with Guidance

Follow structured, mentor-style guidance. Ask questions, debug problems, and improve your project step by step.

4

Publish & Showcase

Ship your project to GitHub, polish write-ups, and translate your work into powerful portfolio & CV outcomes.
